Covid-19 Can't Stop the Music!
We have made many changes in response to COVID-19. Our first priority is the safety of our students and teachers. Because of this, we've decided to discontinue lessons at our physical location and offer online and in-home lessons. In the beginning this seemed unfortunate, as the physical location of Miles Ahead Music was a beautiful community setting to our teaching team and awesome student families. We will continue to offer a “virtual community” through our online portal. We are working on virtual recitals and other fun virtual events to maintain and improve our community! We are excited to offer physical events in the future!

With our new lesson formats we continue to make safety our number one concern. Here are some details about our online and in-home lessons:
Online Lessons
We have come up with an amazing online experience for students and teachers. With our Online Portal, made to accommodate virtual lessons, enjoy access to:
  • Weekly one-on-one live WebEx lessons with an extraordinary teacher
  • Interactive lessons schedule that can be integrated into your own calendar
  • Library of helpful tips and video tutorials
  • Payment information and history
  • Internal messaging system
  • Ability to upload virtual homework assignments (recordings of etudes, scales, repertoire & more)
